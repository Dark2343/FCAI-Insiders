Welcome to the Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence (FCAI),
a leading institution of higher learning dedicated to providing world-class education and research in the fields of computer science and artificial intelligence.

About FCAI

Our faculty is made up of highly qualified and experienced professors, researchers, and staff who are passionate about advancing the frontiers of knowledge in these rapidly evolving fields.
We offer a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs that provide students with the skills, knowledge, and hands-on experience they need to succeed in the digital age.
Students here are taught all the basics of computers and programming in their first 2 years, then are allowed to choose which field they would like to specialize in starting from their 3rd year, where they start learning advanced concepts according to what field they chose.
Our faculty offers internships and training for the students in their last 2 years, and offers a multitude of unique activities throughout their 4 years.

Our Aim

At FCAI, we believe that innovation and collaboration are the keys to success in the 21st century. That's why we encourage our students to work together on projects and research initiatives that tackle some of the most pressing challenges facing society today.
From developing new algorithms for machine learning to designing innovative software applications, our students are at the forefront of cutting-edge research and development.
Our faculty also frequently hosts events from different sources such as: Dell, Intel, etc., to help students get an understanding of the market that awaits them when they graduate.
We also offer multiple clubs that the students can join to socialize and learn from each other while having a safe environment to discuss their hobbies together.

Join Us

Whether you are an aspiring computer scientist or AI researcher, a seasoned professional looking to expand your skills, or simply interested in learning more about these exciting fields,
FCAI is the place for you. Join us today and become part of a dynamic community of innovators and leaders who are shaping the future of technology and society.
